Bio

Our Planet is a nature documentary series made for Netflix.It is a Production of Silver back Flims who also created many other documentary series on BBC like Planet Earth,The Blue Planet and Frozen Planet, with collaboration with World Wildlife Fund(WWF).


Netflix realeased all the episode on 5 April 2019.This series addresses issues of conservation while featuring these disparate animals in their respective home regions.This series was made in order to educate hundreds of millions of people around the world who is needed to protect this beautiful planet.
